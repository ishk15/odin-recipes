Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, then add to batter along with salt.
  3. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  4. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 10 minutes or until edges are nicely browned.
  6. Cool on wire racks.
